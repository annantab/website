Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta that can be used to make a dish of the same name. The dish is made by layering the pasta with other ingredients, such as meat, cheese, and sauce, and baking it. The dish has a savory, rich flavor from the meat sauce and creamy melted cheese, and a tender yet al dente texture from the pasta
